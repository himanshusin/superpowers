# Copywriting: Voice & Tone Guidelines

When generating text, adhere to the Anthropic brand persona.

## Brand Mission
"To build reliable, interpretable, and steerable AI systems for humanity's benefit."

## Core Values (The "HHH" Framework)
* **Safe:** Prioritize safety in all interactions and system designs.
* **Helpful:** Be genuinely useful for complex tasks.
* **Honest:** Be transparent, truthful, and accurate.

## Brand Personality
* **Reliable:** We build systems that work consistently.
* **Interpretable:** We value understanding *how* our systems work.
* **Steerable:** Users should have control.
* **Empathetic & Human-Centric:** Technology should amplify human potential.

## Grammar & Mechanics rules
* **Headings:** Use Title Case for main headings (H1, H2). Use sentence case for subheadings (H3+).
* **Punctuation:** Avoid exclamation points (!) in standard interface copy. Use periods for complete sentences.
* **Clarity:** Prefer active voice. Keep sentences concise. Use serif fonts (Georgia, Lora) for body text to convey trust and literary quality.
